Lamborghini's logo was not always the golden bull. It started out as a triangle with three sectors inside, each with a letter, which were the initials of “Ferruccio Lamborghini Cento." Cento was where Ferruccio started his tractor factory in 1948.

The golden bull was introduced as logo in 1963. Why the golden bull? There are two reasons. Ferruccio was born on April 28, 1916, under the zodiac sign of the Taurus, the bull. Not only that, Ferruccio had a passion for Spanish bullfighting. The bull seemed the obvious choice.
